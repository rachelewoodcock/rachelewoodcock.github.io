Land Surveyor teaching and surveyor CPD:

Izaak de Rijcke is committed to education and the offering of professional learning opportunities for land surveyors in Canada through Four Point Learning. Four Point Learning offers courses, seminars and webinars using distance learning methods and technologies. Its goal is to deliver high-value knowledge and practice for land professionals across Canada, while maintaining ease of access and flexibility. It is also the publisher of Principles of Boundary Law in Canada.
